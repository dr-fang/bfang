Biography

Dr. Bin FANG is an Associate Professor in the Department of Management Science at the School of Management, Xiamen University. He joined Xiamaen University in 2016. He recieved his Ph.D. in Management Science and Engineering (Information Systems major) from School of Management, Harbin Institute of Technology. His current research interests include consumer behaviour in soical influence, social media, online marketplaces, sharing economy. His CV is available here.
